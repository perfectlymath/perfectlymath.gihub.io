Perfectly Math is a composition duo comprised of keyboardist Kyle Robertson and multi-instrumentalist Richard Kentopp. Kyle has written music for many movie trailers and feature length films, and Richard has toured and played as a professional musician in several well known Indie Rock bands. They have long made music with each other in Austin's live music scene, and Perfectly Math is a fresh expression of their musical chemistry, adapted for both the big and small screen.
